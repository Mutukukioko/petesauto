// Tyre Replacement//

Below are steps to replace your car tyre


Use a wrench to loosen the lug nuts on the flat tire. Do not remove them completely yet.

place the Jack lifting onstructions here

Use the wrench to fully remove the lug nuts and set them aside. Then, grasp the tire by the treads and gently pull it toward you to remove it from the wheel base.

Place the spare tire on the wheel base and align the rim with the wheel studs. Push gently until the wheel base touches the brake rotor. Then, screw on the lug nuts by hand as tight as you can.

Use the jack to lower the car back down to the ground. Once the tire is touching the ground, use the wrench to tighten the lug nuts as much as possible. Lower the car completely and remove the jack. Finally, use a torque wrench to tighten the lug nuts to the manufacturer's recommended torque level, which can be found in your car's owner manual.

Double check that the lug nuts are tight and that the spare tire has the correct pressure.

Conclusion

Replacing a flat tyre is a necessary skill for any driver, and can save you time and money if done correctly. Follow these simple steps to safely replace a flat tire:

  • Loosen the lug nuts on the flat tyre
  • Lift the car with a jack
  • Remove the lug nuts and flat tire
  • Mount the spare tire and tighten the lug nuts
  • Lower the car and finish tightening the lug nuts

Remember to always consult your vehicle's owner's manual for specific instructions and safety precautions. If you're unsure about any step of the process, don't hesitate to seek professional help.

In the conclusion, we summarize the main steps of replacing a flat tire and emphasize the importance of following safety guidelines and consulting the owner's manual. We also encourage readers to seek professional help if they feel unsure about any aspect of the process.
